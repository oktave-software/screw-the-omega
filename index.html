SCREW THE OMEGA

Screw the Omega is an independent webcomic about Neji Okalu, a Nigerian-born, American raised first-year college student, who moonlights as a demon hunter in New York City. As an urban fantasy, the story centers around supernatural entities and how they impact the lives of young BIPOC in the inner city.
